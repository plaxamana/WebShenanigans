Carlton Banks

Carlton Banks has sold luxury Real Estate on the Westside of Los Angeles since 1979. Over the past 35 years he has built a successful career and a reputation by helping families and individuals buy and sell their luxury homes. Carlton has built his business representing discerning clients who own high-end properties in some of the most exclusive communities in Toronto. His strong referral network, repeat clientele and high level of integrity is a large part of why he is always ranked among the top agents nationally. Carlton works successfully in very competitive and fast-paced luxury markets. He is professional, discreet, and easily adjusts to new marketing trends, using his experience to assist his clients in making the best choices when buying, selling, or making investments. A skilled negotiator with a proven track record, Carlton has closed over $1 billion in Real Estate transactions. His clients include professionals, celebrities and international high net-worth individuals who appreciate his experience and local expertise.

Mission Statement
